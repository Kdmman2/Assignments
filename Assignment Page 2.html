The condos at Destin Trails

What is a condo?

Our luxury condos are permanent structures four feet off the gound. Each condo has canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.

How are the condos furnished?

Each condo is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. Your luxury camping experience includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.

What should I bring?

Most guests pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with light layers of clothing. It's also helpful to bring a flashlight and a sense of adventure!
